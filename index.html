FAQ

Questions firms ask before signing up

Is my client's financial data secure?+
Yes, completely. Every file you upload is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your client's data is used solely to generate the report, then permanently and automatically deleted within 30 days. We never retain it beyond that window, never use it for any other purpose, and never share it with third parties. We are fully UK GDPR compliant.
How do I get started?+
Create your account at app.pagevo.co.uk — it takes under a minute. Then export your client's P&L from Xero, QuickBooks, Sage, or Excel as a CSV and upload it through your dashboard. Your first report is completely free. You don't need to enter payment details until you've seen the output and decided to subscribe.
What format does my data need to be in?+
A standard P&L export from Xero, QuickBooks, Sage, or Excel — as a CSV or .xlsx file. In Xero, that's Reports → Profit and Loss → Export. We also provide a simple upload template if you'd prefer to enter figures directly. If you're unsure exactly what to export, email us at reports@pagevo.co.uk and we'll tell you precisely.
How long does it take to receive the report?+
Typically under 2 minutes from uploading your data. You'll receive a confirmation and the finished PDF arrives in your inbox shortly after.
Can I put my firm's branding on the reports?+
Yes — the White Label plan puts your firm's name, logo, brand colours, tagline, contact details, and legal disclaimer on every report. The PDF filename also includes your firm name. Your clients receive a polished document from your firm. Pagevo doesn't appear anywhere — on the report, in the filename, or in any client communication.
What is the client portal?+
All subscribers access a dashboard at app.pagevo.co.uk to submit reports and manage their account. White Label subscribers also get a complete report history — every report you've ever generated, downloadable as PDFs. It's your permanent record of every client report you've sent.
Does it work for any type of business?+
Yes. The report reads the data and adapts its language and benchmarks to the business type — a restaurant gets hospitality-relevant commentary, a consulting firm gets service-sector commentary. You don't configure anything; the system works it out from the figures.
Can I cancel at any time?+
Yes — month-to-month, no contracts, no cancellation fees. Email reports@pagevo.co.uk at any time and we'll deactivate your account at the end of your current billing period. You keep full access until the period ends.
How is this different from Syft or Fathom?+
Three meaningful differences. Pricing: Syft and Fathom charge per connected client — typically £18–£25/client/month, which scales against you as your practice grows. Pagevo is one flat monthly fee regardless of client count. Setup: Syft and Fathom require your clients to grant OAuth access to their accounting software — some clients refuse. We need only a CSV export you already produce. Scope: we focus entirely on the client-facing PDF report. That's the deliverable your clients actually receive and read.
Do my clients need to do anything?+
Nothing at all. Your clients don't sign up, don't grant software access, and receive no communications from Pagevo. You export the data, upload it, receive the PDF, and forward it. To your client, it's a professional monthly report from your firm — delivered reliably, every time.
What happens to my client data after the report is generated?+
It's permanently deleted within 30 days of report delivery — automatically, with no manual process. We use the data solely to generate your report. We don't train on it, don't analyse it across clients, and don't share it with anyone. This meets UK GDPR requirements and is documented in full in our privacy policy.
