FAQ

Common questions

How do I get started?
Register your account using our onboarding form, then submit your first client's P&L data using the report submission form. Your first report is free — no payment required until you're happy with the output.
What format does my data need to be in?
Any CSV or Excel export from Xero, QuickBooks, Sage, or any spreadsheet software. We provide a template if you're unsure. The more detail you include, the richer the report — but even basic revenue and profit figures produce a useful output.
How long does it take to receive the report?
Typically within 15 minutes of uploading your data. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately and the finished PDF shortly after.
Is my client's financial data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We are UK GDPR compliant and data is permanently deleted within 30 days of report delivery. We never sell or share client data with third parties.
Can I put my firm's branding on the reports?
Yes — that's what our White Label plan is for. Your firm name, logo, brand colours, tagline, contact details, and legal disclaimer all appear on every report. Your clients see your brand, not FinReportAI.
Does it work for any type of business?
Yes. The report adapts automatically to the business type — a restaurant report uses different language and highlights different metrics to a consulting firm or retail business. No manual configuration required.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Just email us and we'll deactivate your account at the end of your current billing period.
